---

## About Camp Synthesis

Camp placement: **B and 5:45**

The Camp has a Japanese theme, focused on Sushi, Sake, and Synths. We have DJ decks and a sound system. We have synths. We run public food and bar events. We have a group from Osaka in camp.

---

## We're Looking For

- People to get involved with the events (1–2 weeks)
- Help sharing logistics costs
- Help with set up and tear down
- People to bring their international flavor

---

## More About Camp

We don't have dues if you get involved. If you just want to show up, we ask for **$500 per camper** in advance to cover infrastructure costs (sake, sushi, logistics).

Please plan to be **self sufficient**. We don't run the sushi bar as a cafeteria for camp — it's too complicated. Bring your food. If you help with events, there is always food available.
